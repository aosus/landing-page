The September 2022 edition of the Aosus Writing Contest has concluded with five excellent winning entries!

This month's selection includes a diverse range of topics, with a particular focus on web technologies and their intersection with the open-source ecosystem. The articles cover frameworks, self-hosting solutions, and privacy-focused web tools.

We continue to see growing participation in the contest, which is a testament to the Arabic FOSS community's dedication to creating high-quality content.

Thank you to all participants for their valuable contributions to the Arabic open-source knowledge base.
