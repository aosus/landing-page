We are excited to announce that the Aosus community is now fiscally sponsored by The Hack Foundation (d.b.a. Hack Club), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

This partnership means that donations to Aosus are now tax-deductible in the United States. It also provides us with a solid financial infrastructure through Hack Club Bank (HCB), ensuring full transparency in how community funds are managed.

All financial transactions are publicly visible on our HCB page, reflecting our commitment to transparency and accountability in how we handle community resources.

This milestone allows us to better support our mission of promoting free and open-source software in the Arabic world, fund community events, and maintain our privacy-respecting services.
