10:T435,<p>Aosus community is pleased to announce its sponsorship of the Daydream Alexandria event, to be held in Alexandria, Egypt.</p>
<p>Daydream is a global hackathon organized by Hack Club (HCB), the fiscal sponsor of the Aosus community. The hackathon targets school students to introduce them to the basics of programming through game development.</p>
<p>The event in Alexandria will focus specifically on free software, using open-source game engines like Godot, and encouraging the use of community and open resources instead of proprietary solutions. Participants will also upload their games to GitHub and learn how to share and publish them on itch.io.</p>
<p>Through this sponsorship, Aosus community aims to support student movements working to spread the culture of free software in the Arab world, increase student awareness of its importance, and introduce them to it at early stages of their software journey.</p>
<p>It is worth noting that the Aosus community has previously supported the organizing team in the Alexandria edition of the Scrapyard Vanity event.</p>
