Piped Service Discontinuation

FarisZR2025-08-15

The Piped service hosted by the Aosus community has been facing persistent playback issues over the past few months. After careful consideration, we have decided to announce the upcoming discontinuation of this service.

Piped is a privacy-respecting frontend for YouTube that allows users to watch videos without tracking. However, due to frequent changes in YouTube's infrastructure, maintaining a reliable Piped instance has become increasingly challenging.

We want to assure our community that all other privacy services hosted by Aosus — including SearXNG, Simply Translate, Redlib, and others — will continue to operate as usual.

We encourage our users to explore alternative Piped instances maintained by other communities, or to consider using other privacy-respecting YouTube frontends.

Thank you for your understanding and continued support of the Aosus community.
