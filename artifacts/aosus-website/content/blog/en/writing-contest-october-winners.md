We are pleased to announce the winners of the Aosus Writing Contest for October 2022!

This month saw an impressive collection of detailed and well-researched topics about free and open-source software. The oversight committee has selected five outstanding articles that demonstrate deep knowledge and passion for the FOSS ecosystem.

The winning articles cover a range of topics from Linux system administration to privacy tools and open-source alternatives to proprietary software.

Congratulations to all the winners! Your contributions help enrich the Arabic content about free software and inspire others in the community.

The winning articles will be republished on the GnulinuxSA blog with full author attribution, as per the contest rules.
